What is NBTC?

Fuelled by a passion for next-generation technology.

For the past 18 years, The National Business and Technology Conference has been Canada’s premiere destination for professionals in the business and technology industry.

It’s a gathering of young leaders, CEOs, hackers, thinkers and hustlers. A congregation of the industry’s innovators and the upcoming generation of founders, entrepreneurs and leaders to share and celebrate the innovation and diversity that makes the technology community thrive NBTC is hosted and attended by people who not only understand technology they live and breathe it.

Each year at NBTC, hundreds of students take advantage of the opportunity to network with recruiters, participate in competitions, and learn about the critical technologies that are being used in modern businesses.

Think IoT, SaaS, big data, deep learning and AI. Not familiar? Come to NBTC to learn more about the technologies companies rely on. Our workshops will provide you the help you need to build a compelling case to impress our sponsors.

Who Goes?

NBTC isn't just for students enrolled in business and STEM programs. Anyone with an interest in the cutting edge of technology, and its intersection with the business world, can take advantage of an incredibly unique experience at NBTC. Here's who you can expect to see at NBTC:

Talent
The brightest and best students and prospective biztech wizards around, all in search of the next big thing, and the opportunity to network with some of the biggest and most exciting brands. We attract talent from schools all over southern Ontario, with attendees from U of T, UW, Queen's, UWO, York, and Ryerson.
Entrepreneurs
People with big ideas and a strong interest in the cutting edge of their industry often find themselves at home at NBTC. Passionate, hard-working, and always up to a new challenge, these are the faces of our fabled case competitions and entrepreneurship challenges.
Industry Leaders
NBTC is your chance to get to know some of the minds behind the country's most successful startups, firms, and institutions. See our list of past speakers below for a glimpse of who you might just bump into this year.
